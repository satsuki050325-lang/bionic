# BIONIC_PRODUCT

## Product
- Product name: `Bionic`
- Core runner: `Bionic Engine`

## Core Idea
- A self-operating foundation for an individual developer running multiple services.
- Human remains the decision-maker.
- AI supports observation, prioritization, and explanation.

## Initial Shape
- `Bionic Engine`: local independent background runner
- `@bionic/sdk`: thin integration client
- `Bionic CLI`: terminal operations
- `Bionic App`: desktop-first UI, initially web-based

## Phase 1 Success
- Medini sends health events through the SDK.
- Important research arrives as a daily digest.
- Alerts are visible in one screen.
- The system is worth checking daily even without self-repair.
